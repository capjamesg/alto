Welcome to SnackAuth

What is SnackAuth?

SnackAuth is a service that lets you authenticate to a website with your domain name.

SnackAuth works using IndieAuth. To sign in, you must first add rel=me links to your site. Then, SnackAuth will present you with authentication options based on your rel=me links.

Once you have signed in with an authentication option, you can authorize a service to sign you in as your domain name.

SnackAuth is not currently open for registrations.

If you are looking for a hosted IndieAuth solution, check out the "Set up using IndieAuth" tutorial on the IndieWeb wiki.
